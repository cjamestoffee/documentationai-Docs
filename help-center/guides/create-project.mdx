## Overview

Get started by making your first project for documentation in Next Step Conrad.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to https://nextstepconrad.com and log into your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter a name and (optionally) a description for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and add pages">
    Click "Create" and start adding your documentation pages right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>
