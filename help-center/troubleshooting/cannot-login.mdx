## Symptoms

- Login button doesn't work
- Error: "Invalid credentials"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot Password?" to reset and follow the link in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser issues">
    Try clearing cache or use a private window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still stuck?">
    Contact support at support@nextstepconrad.com
  </Step>
</Steps>
