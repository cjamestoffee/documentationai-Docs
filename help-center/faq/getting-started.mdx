## Overview

Getting started with Next Step Conrad is easy. Projects help organize your docs.

## Steps

1. Log in to your Next Step Conrad dashboard
2. Click "New Project"
3. Name your project (e.g., "Team Wiki")
4. Click "Create"
5. Start adding pages to document your work

See more in the Guides or contact support if you get stuck.
